This contest is about learning, making friends, and, most importantly, having fun! Check out the Challenges page for more information.

Challenges:

This contest will consist of 6 challenges, each lasting 2 weeks, with the exception of the homepage challenge (which is one week).
You may submit entries until 11:59 PM, GMT, of the last day available per challenge.

2 Jan - 9 Jan

Everybody will make a homepage for their team. The page can be whatever design ya like, and on either PJS or HTML. The creator of the homepage with the post points will be team leader, and the homepage will be the place for your team to meet up and talk in.

9 Jan - 23 Jan

Make something winter-themed! Or, well, whatever season it is on your side of the world, ig! :D

23 Jan - 6 Feb

[...First of all, wowwww very creative naming, Cardstock woowww ooooh XD]

2nd off, make something that's fun! Anything! From a graphic to a game to even a webpage, if you feel so inclined... Fun! Funnnnnn! FUN!

6 Feb - 20 Feb

Try to learn something new and make a program with it!

20 Feb - 6 Mar

Pick a teammate from your team who you don't really know and do a collaboration with them! It's totally okay if more than two people are doing the collab (even if you end up knowing some of them) :D

6 Mar - 20 Mar

You and your team'll work together to make three programs.

Join:

You have until 1st January to join.
To join, please answer the questions below:

  • What skill level would you consider yourself?
    (beginner, familiar, intermediate, advanced, expert)
  • What team would you like to be on?
    (please be aware, if we have too many competitors on one team, you may be switched out to another one)

    (just give us an estimate)
